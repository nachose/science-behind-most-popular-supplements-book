# Chapter 4: Probiotics

## Chapter Outline

- **Defining probiotics**  
  Strains, live cultures, and gut microbiome basics.

- **Mechanisms of action**  
  Modulation of gut flora, immune modulation, and digestive health.

- **Scientific evidence**  
  Trials on IBS, antibiotic recovery, and mental health via gut-brain axis.

- **Potential drawbacks**  
  Strain-specific effects, overgrowth risks, and quality variability.

- **Usage recommendations**  
  CFU counts, specific conditions, and prebiotic synergies.

- **Future research**  
  Personalized microbiomics and long-term effects.
