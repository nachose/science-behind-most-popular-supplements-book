# Chapter 10: Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10)

## Chapter Outline

- **What is CoQ10**  
  Ubiquinone/ubiquinol, mitochondrial function, and endogenous production.

- **Biological importance**  
  Energy production, antioxidant defense, and statin-related depletion.

- **Research evidence**  
  Trials on heart failure, migraine, and fertility.

- **Risks and interactions**  
  Blood pressure effects, quality standards, and synthesis decline with age.

- **Optimal intake**  
  Forms, doses for specific conditions, and bioavailability enhancers.

- **Clinical applications**  
  In cardiology and anti-aging research.
