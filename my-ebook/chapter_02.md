# Chapter 2: Vitamin D

## Chapter Outline

- **What is Vitamin D**  
  Sources, forms (D2 vs. D3), and synthesis in the body.

- **Physiological roles**  
  Bone health, immune function, and cellular processes.

- **Research overview**  
  Clinical trials on deficiency prevention, mood regulation, and chronic disease risk reduction.

- **Risks and considerations**  
  Toxicity, testing for levels, and factors affecting absorption like sunlight exposure.

- **Optimal intake**  
  Recommended doses, supplementation strategies, and population-specific advice.

- **Emerging science**  
  Links to autoimmune conditions and long-term health outcomes.
